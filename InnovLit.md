**4. Service Description (In 2350 Characters incl. spaces):**

*   **Introduction**
    "Fundamentals of Innovation Literacy" workshop equips teams with the foundational skills to drive impactful change. Learn to effectively surface high-value ideas and de-risk process improvements, product development, or technology adoption through structured methods. This comprehensive one-day session demystifies the innovation journey, providing practical tools and fostering a collaborative mindset crucial for navigating change and succeeding within the Irish manufacturing context.

*   **What to expect?**
    Participants will engage in a full-day interactive session covering the core components of Innovation Literacy. We explore structured innovation processes, focusing on Design Thinking with practical manufacturing examples. The workshop delves into essential innovation tools and techniques, including strategic, problem-framing, ideation, and evaluation methods. We cover the vital role of digital tools, including collaborative platforms and AI technologies relevant to Industry 5.0. Cultivating key innovation mindsets and understanding the importance of effective facilitation and resource allocation are central themes. 

*   **What will your Service Users takeaway after the service?**
    Attendees will leave with:
    *   A clear understanding of structured innovation processes, particularly Design Thinking, and how to apply them.
    *   Knowledge of a range of traditional and digital innovation tools (including AI) applicable to manufacturing challenges.
    *   An appreciation for the critical mindsets needed to drive innovation.
    *   Insight into the role of facilitation and the resources required for successful innovation initiatives.
    *   A foundational framework for integrating innovation literacy into their Industry 5.0 transition strategy.
    *   Actionable insights to foster innovation within their teams and organisation.
    *   Access to a comprehensive suite of supplementary resources.
