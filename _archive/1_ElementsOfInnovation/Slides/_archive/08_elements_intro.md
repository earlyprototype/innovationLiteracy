Section 1: Elements of Innovation

### Text
Nine core components that underpin effective innovation practice.

**Structural:**
1.1 Process | 1.2 Interventions | 1.3 Tools

**Individual:**
1.4 Skills & Competencies | 1.5 Mindset | 1.6 Behaviours

**Collective & Contextual:**
1.7 Teams | 1.8 Facilitation | 1.9 Environment

### Speaker Notes
This is a section header slide -- use it to give participants a map of where they are going. The three groupings (structural, individual, collective/contextual) are not rigid categories but a useful way to see the progression: from how the work is structured, through what individuals need, to how teams and organisations create the conditions for it. Signal that Session 1 will cover the structural elements and Session 2 will pick up with the individual and collective elements. The closing line -- "innovation capability is systemic" -- is the key message of this entire section.
