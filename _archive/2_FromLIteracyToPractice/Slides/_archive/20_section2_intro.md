## Slide Title
Section 2: From Literacy to Practice

### Text
Understanding innovation is necessary but not sufficient.

#### The Transition
- **Section 0**: What innovation is and why it matters
- **Section 1**: The elements that make it work
- **Section 2**: How to embed it as a sustained organisational practice

#### Three Steps
2.1 Permission Through Understanding
2.2 Co-creating an Innovation Charter
2.3 Demonstrating Value

Without deliberate action to bridge this gap, workshop learning remains theoretical.

### Speaker Notes
This is a section header that reorients participants. They have spent the majority of the workshop building understanding; this final section addresses the practical question of "so what do we do with this?" The three steps are sequential and cumulative: you need informed leadership support (2.1) before you can co-create a charter (2.2), and you need both before you can demonstrate value effectively (2.3). Signal that this section is shorter but arguably the most practically important -- it is where the workshop connects to their organisational reality.
