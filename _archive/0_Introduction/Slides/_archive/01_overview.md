# Fundamentals of Innovation Literacy - Slide 2

--- CONTENT

## Slide Title
Workshop Overview

### Text
#### What This Workshop Addresses
- Innovation as a *process* -- structured, repeatable, learnable
- Innovation *management* -- the organisational capability to sustain it
- The transition from understanding to embedding innovation as practice

#### Session Structure
- Session 1: Introduction and Elements of Innovation (structural)
- Session 2: Elements of Innovation (human and organisational) and From Literacy to Practice

#### How We Will Work
- Presentation and discussion in equal measure
- Your context and experience are as important as the material
- Questions encouraged throughout

### Speaker Notes
Frame the two levels immediately -- process and management. Signal that this is not a passive lecture: their participation is expected and valued. Briefly outline the two-session structure and where the natural break falls. Emphasise that the workshop operates at both levels because organisations that invest in one without the other consistently fail to sustain innovation.
