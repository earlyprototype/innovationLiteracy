What We are Going to do in this Workshop
''Understanding Design as process for effective innovation to enable people to work efficient through creative collaboration to create real world impact that is sustainable.''

--

"You'll learn innovation by doing it badly, then doing it well, then understanding why the difference matters - so you can spot cargo-culting, protect real innovation work from operational gravity, and build the capability in your organisation without the typical 70% failure rate.

By the end of this you will have learned to recognise the key elements of innovation and have a sense of why they are important and when they come into play. To fully develop your innovation literacy you will have to embed and practice process-led innovation into your work."
