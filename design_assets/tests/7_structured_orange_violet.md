# Prompt: Card Design Test 7 — Structured Burnt Orange + Violet

**Target Image Ratio:** 16:9  

**System Instruction:**
You are an expert Information Designer and Editorial Layout specialist. Generate a Super-Resolution 4K (3840×2160) high-fidelity 16:9 infographic. This is a flat digital UI graphic, edge-to-edge. Treat this as a premium editorial page layout — every element earns its place through purpose and spatial intent. Use absolute typographic precision and zero compression artifacts.

---

## Typography System (CRITICAL — follow exactly)

- **Title:** Refined bold serif (Playfair Display or similar editorial serif), 60–72pt, left-aligned in the header zone. Generous letterspacing (+2%).
- **Subtitle:** Humanist sans-serif (Inter or similar), light weight, 28–32pt, positioned directly beneath title with 8px gap.
- **Section Labels** ("THE CRAFT", "THE FORMULA"): Sans-serif, medium weight, small-caps, 18–20pt, burnt orange (#E25C2B) colour.
- **Body Text:** Sans-serif, regular weight, 16–18pt, line-height 1.6. Dark Ink (#1A1A1A).
- **Example/Quote Text:** Sans-serif, italic, 15pt, indented with a thin orange left-border rule (2px).
- **Caption / Metadata text:** Sans-serif, light weight, 11–12pt, upper-case letterspaced.

## Compositional Structure (CRITICAL — follow this spatial grid)

The layout is divided into three horizontal zones on a matte off-white/light sand background (#F5F5F0):

### Header Zone (top 15% of frame)
- Title "Define" sits **left-aligned**, 72px from left edge, 48px from top.
- A thin burnt orange underline (2px, extending 50% of title width) sits beneath it.
- Subtitle "Closing the first diamond." sits below the underline, left-aligned.
- Top-right corner: small metadata label "DESIGN THINKING / PROCESS" in caption style, deep violet (#5A3B8C).

### Content Zone (middle 65% of frame — asymmetric two-column: 55% left / 45% right)

**Left Column (55%) — The Visual Metaphor:**
The "Chaos to Clarity" visual lives here. Dense scattered translucent post-it notes and data points in Deep Violet (#5A3B8C) at varying opacities (10%–60%) form the "chaos wall" on the far left. These converge via thin structural lines toward the centre, distilling into a single sharp horizontal burnt orange bar (#E25C2B) that extends into the right column.

**Right Column (45%) — The Content Stack:**
A clean vertical stack of text blocks, each clearly separated by 24px whitespace:

1. **Main Body:** "Synthesis transforms chaos into clarity." — body text style, sitting at the top of this column.
2. **THE CRAFT** (section label, orange small-caps, with a thin orange rule beneath):
   "How Might We..." statements.
   - Below: example quote in italic with left orange border:
     "HMW help employees build trusted relationships?"
3. **THE FORMULA** (section label, orange small-caps, with thin orange rule beneath):
   [User] + [Need] + [Insight] — displayed as a horizontal equation with each bracketed term in medium weight and the + signs in light weight violet.

### Footer Zone (bottom 20% of frame)
Empty whitespace with only a thin violet hairline rule at the very bottom edge. This breathing room is intentional — editorial restraint.

- **Secondary Accent:** Deep Violet (#5A3B8C) — for chaos nodes, post-it notes, structural lines, metadata.
- **Text Colour:** Dark Ink (#1A1A1A).

- Must be a flat 2D vector-style infographic. No 3D objects. No photographs.
