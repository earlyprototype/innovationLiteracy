**System Instruction:**
You are an expert Information Designer and Editorial Layout specialist. Generate a Super-Resolution 4K (3840×2160) high-fidelity 16:9 infographic. This is a flat digital UI graphic, edge-to-edge. ## Typography System (CRITICAL — follow exactly)

- **Title:** Refined bold serif (Playfair Display or similar editorial serif), 60–72pt, left-aligned in the header zone. Generous letterspacing (+2%).
- **Subtitle:** Humanist sans-serif (Inter or similar), light weight, 28–32pt, positioned directly beneath title with 8px gap.
- **Section Labels** ("THE CRAFT", "THE FORMULA"): Sans-serif, medium weight, small-caps, 18–20pt, electric coral (#FF6B6B) colour.
- **Body Text:** Sans-serif, regular weight, 16–18pt, line-height 1.6. Pitch Black (#111111).
- **Example/Quote Text:** Sans-serif, italic, 15pt, indented with a thin coral left-border rule (2px).
- **Caption / Metadata text:** Sans-serif, light weight, 11–12pt, upper-case letterspaced.

The layout is divided into three horizontal zones on a stark cool white background (#F8F9FA):

### Header Zone (top 15% of frame)
- Title "Define" sits **left-aligned**, 72px from left edge, 48px from top.
- A thin electric coral underline (2px, extending 50% of title width) sits beneath it.
- Subtitle "Closing the first diamond." sits below the underline, left-aligned.
- Top-right corner: small metadata label "DESIGN THINKING / PROCESS" in caption style, vibrant teal (#00E5FF).

### Content Zone (middle 65% of frame — asymmetric two-column: 55% left / 45% right)

**Left Column (55%) — The Visual Metaphor:**
The "Chaos to Clarity" visual lives here. Dense scattered translucent post-it notes and data points in Vibrant Teal (#00E5FF) at varying opacities (10%–60%) form the "chaos wall" on the far left. These converge via thin structural lines toward the centre, distilling into a single sharp horizontal electric coral bar (#FF6B6B) that extends into the right column.

**Right Column (45%) — The Content Stack:**
A clean vertical stack of text blocks, each clearly separated by 24px whitespace:

1. **Main Body:** "Synthesis transforms chaos into clarity." — body text style, sitting at the top of this column.
2. **THE CRAFT** (section label, coral small-caps, with a thin coral rule beneath):
   "How Might We..." statements.
   - Below: example quote in italic with left coral border:
     "HMW help employees build trusted relationships?"
3. **THE FORMULA** (section label, coral small-caps, with thin coral rule beneath):
   [User] + [Need] + [Insight] — displayed as a horizontal equation with each bracketed term in medium weight and the + signs in light weight teal.

### Footer Zone (bottom 20% of frame)
Empty whitespace with only a thin teal hairline rule at the very bottom edge. This breathing room is intentional — editorial restraint.
