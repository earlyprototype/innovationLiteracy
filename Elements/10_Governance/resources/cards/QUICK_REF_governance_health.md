# Quick Reference: Governance Health Check

## Red Flag Checklist

- [ ] Innovation time has been "borrowed" for operational work in the last month
- [ ] Stakeholders are asking for ROI before discovery is complete
- [ ] A failed experiment was treated as someone's failure, not team learning
- [ ] Process stages were skipped under pressure
- [ ] Innovation activities happen but outputs are ignored
- [ ] The innovation champion is doing all the advocacy alone

**Score: 0 flags** = healthy | **1-2** = attention needed | **3+** = governance intervention required

## Quick Diagnostic Questions

1. "When was the last time innovation time was protected from an operational deadline?"
2. "What learning metrics are we tracking at this stage?"
3. "When did we last celebrate an experiment that failed?"
