# ⚡ QUICK REF: Four Gates Checklist

Run after **every** AI generation. Takes 30 seconds.

| # | Gate | Ask | Red Flag |
|---|------|-----|----------|
| 1 | **Alignment** | Did it answer my actual question? | Confident answer to wrong question |
| 2 | **Tension** | Are trade-offs visible? | Suspiciously perfect solution |
| 3 | **Source Grounding** | Can I trace claims to sources? | "Most users..." without data |
| 4 | **Bias** | Is it challenging my assumptions? | Confirms everything you believe |

## If Any Gate Fails

**Don't accept.** Diagnose → Refine prompt → Regenerate → Compare.

## Stage Emphasis

- **Discovery:** Gates 3, 4
- **Define:** Gates 1, 4
- **Ideate:** Gates 2, 1
- **Prototype:** Gates 1, 2
- **Test:** Gates 3, 2
