# Exercise: The User Manual of Me

**Source:** Cassie Robinson

## Why It Matters

Cognitive diversity is the raw material of innovation but naturally produces friction. If team members don't understand *how* their colleagues operate, necessary cognitive friction devolves into interpersonal conflict.

## Key Effects

- **Proactive empathy:** Forces empathising with teammates *before* work gets stressful
- **Operationalises neurodiversity:** Provides structured way for neurodivergent/introverted members to state needs without awkwardly asking in the moment
- **Clarifies communication:** Removes guesswork from collaboration

## Running the Exercise

1. **Pre-work:** Before a long-term project begins, each member fills out a one-page template
2. **The Prompts:**
   - "The best way to communicate with me is..."
   - "The times/situations where I do my best work are..."
   - "I get stressed when..."
   - "How to tell if I'm stressed..."
   - "The best way to give me feedback is..."
3. **The Review:** First 30 minutes of kickoff — team reads each other's manuals
4. **Persistence:** Keep pinned to wall or top of digital workspace as ongoing reference
