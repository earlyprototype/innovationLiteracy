# Quick Reference: Bias Countermeasures

| Bias | Symptom | Countermeasure |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **Confirmation** | Testing to prove, not learn | Pre-defined success/failure criteria |
| **Sunk Cost** | "We've invested too much to stop" | Stage-gate with metered funding |
| **Availability** | Last interview dominates | Systematic synthesis, structured prioritisation |
| **Groupthink** | Everyone agrees too easily | Anonymous input first, devil's advocate role |
| **IKEA Effect** | Team loves their own prototype | Creator-observer separation, "don't defend" |

## Quick Diagnostic

Ask these three questions:
1. **"What evidence would make us stop?"** (Tests for confirmation bias)
2. **"If we started today, would we still do this?"** (Tests for sunk cost)
3. **"Who hasn't spoken yet?"** (Tests for groupthink)
