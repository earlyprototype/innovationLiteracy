# Facilitation: Expanded Ideation

**Use case:** Generate wide range of solutions, avoid premature convergence.

## Facilitation Moves

### 1. Generate Human Ideas First (10 min)
- Traditional brainstorming, no AI
- Participants generate 10-15 core concepts
- Document on board

### 2. Feed Best Concepts to AI (5 min)
- Select 3-5 strongest human ideas as seeds
- Prompt: "Generate variations that maintain [user needs] and [constraints]"
- AI produces expanded set (50-100 ideas)

### 3. Narrow Strategically (5 min)
- Facilitator or small group filters against evaluation criteria
- "Show only those that [meet criteria]"
- Curate to manageable set (15-20 ideas)

### 4. Present Curated Set (10 min)
- Show original human ideas + selected AI variations
- Facilitate evaluation and selection

## Key Language

- "We start with human thinking, then use AI to expand"
- "AI gave us 100 variations — I've narrowed to these 20 based on [criteria]"
- "Which of these — human or AI-expanded — merit prototyping?"

## Failure Mode Prevented

Overwhelming participants with 100 AI ideas (drowning in output). Skipping human creative thinking entirely (premature convergence).

## Time: ~30 min total
(10 human + 5 AI + 5 curation + 10 evaluation)
