# Quick Reference: Process Navigator

## Which Phase Am I In?

| Phase | Question | What's Permitted | What's Banned |
|-------|----------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Empathise** | What do humans need? | Observation, inquiry, "why?" | Solutions, assumptions |
| **Define** | What's the real problem? | Multiple framings, reframing | Jumping to "how" |
| **Ideate** | What could we do? | Wild ideas, quantity, "yes and" | Evaluation, "yes but" |
| **Prototype** | How might this work? | Quick builds, disposable tests | Polish, attachment |
| **Test** | Does this actually work? | Evidence gathering, listening | Defending, explaining |

## Diamond Check

```
Am I in the FIRST diamond (problem space) or SECOND (solution space)?
Am I OPENING (diverging) or CLOSING (converging)?
```

## Mode Violations — Catch Phrases

| Violation | Phrase to Watch For | Correction |
|-----------|-------------------|------------|
| Evaluating during divergence | "That won't work" | "Hold that — we're still diverging" |
| Solving during empathise | "We should just..." | "We're understanding, not solving yet" |
| Converging without exploring | "Let's just pick one" | "We need more options first" |
| Defending during test | "But that's because..." | "Don't defend your prototype" |
