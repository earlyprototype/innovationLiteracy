# ⚡ QUICK REF: Verification Language

## Ownership Language (Use These)

✅ "AI spotted these patterns — which ones resonate with what you observed?"
✅ "AI generated options — which serves our users best and why?"
✅ "AI synthesised this feedback — what does it mean for our next prototype?"
✅ "You own the interpretation, not the AI"

## Outsourcing Language (Avoid These)

❌ "AI says we should do X"
❌ "The data shows..." (it's AI interpretation)
❌ "According to this analysis..." (who analysed — AI or you?)

## Verification Phrases

- "Before we accept this, let's verify it"
- "Can someone trace this claim back to source material?"
- "This looks perfect. That's suspicious."
- "Where are the trade-offs we're not seeing?"
- "I'm getting echo chamber vibes — is this challenging our assumptions?"

## Failure Reframing

- "This nonsense is useful — what did we fail to specify?"
- "What does this output reveal about our prompt logic?"
- "Each prompt iteration teaches us about articulating intent"

## Resistance Responses

| They Say | You Say |
|----------|---------|
| "This slows us down" | "30 seconds now saves hours later" |
| "I trust AI" | "Trust requires verification" |
| "It looks perfect" | "That's when verification matters most" |
| "No time" | "Verify now or fix downstream — choose" |
