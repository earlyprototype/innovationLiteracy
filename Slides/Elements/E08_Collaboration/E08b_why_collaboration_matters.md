# E08b — Collaboration: Why It Matters

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Collaboration?

### Body
Cognitive demands are distributed. Without it, diversity is just confusion. With it, cognitive friction becomes productive energy.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: Unmanaged diversity (arrows pointing in all directions, intersecting chaotically, causing friction without movement). Right side: Facilitated collaboration (arrows aligned in parallel or weaving together to form a strong, forward-moving vector). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the cohesive structure.
