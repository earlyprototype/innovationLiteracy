# E08 — Collaboration: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Collaboration vs Team Composition

### Body
Interpersonal competence, not who is on the team

### Visual Direction
Comparison layout. Collaboration: how people work together (psychological safety, role clarity, productive tension). Team composition: who is in the room (separate concern). Psychological safety is not "being nice" — safe teams have rigorous debate. Role clarity within collaboration covers dynamic role negotiation, not formal archetypes.
