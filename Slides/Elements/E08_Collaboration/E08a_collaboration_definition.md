# E08a — Collaboration: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What is Collaboration?

### Body
Interpersonal competence that enables diverse individuals to work together productively. Integration of diverse knowledge, perspectives, and skills.

### Visual Direction
Structural diagram showing multiple distinct inputs converging into a single strong output. Neon yellow (#DFFF00) lines representing diverse perspectives coming together into a woven or interlocking central node. Clean architectural diagram style representing synergy, not just proximity.
