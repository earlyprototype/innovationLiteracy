# E08d — Collaboration: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Psychological Safety vs Being Nice

### Body
Being nice is avoiding conflict to protect feelings. Psychological safety is embracing rigorous debate without attacking people.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Being Nice (shown as a padded, restrictive box where ideas cannot interact or spark). Right: Psychological Safety (shown as an open arena where ideas collide and spark, but the boundaries protect the participants). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the productive collision of ideas.
