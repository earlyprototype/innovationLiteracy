# E02 — Interventions: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Interventions vs Tools

### Body
Tools are what you use. Interventions are how you are told to use them.

### Visual Direction
Clear comparison layout. Mural is a tool; "draw your ideal innovation process in 5 minutes" is an intervention. Pencil is a tool; "sketch, don't write" is an intervention. Stage-dependent: each Design Thinking phase has specific interventions. Three words can change everything — "dig deeper" transforms discovery quality.
