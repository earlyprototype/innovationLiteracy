# E09a — Facilitation: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What is Facilitation?

### Body
The practice of guiding a group through a process. Enabling participation, managing dynamics, and capturing learning. The synthesis capability.

### Visual Direction
Structural diagram showing an orchestrator role. A central neon yellow (#DFFF00) node or hub that doesn't produce the content itself, but rather directs, aligns, and balances the flow of information between other nodes. Clean architectural metaphor for guidance rather than creation.
