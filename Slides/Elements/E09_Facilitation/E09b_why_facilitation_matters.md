# E09b — Facilitation: Why It Matters

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Facilitation?

### Body
Without it, teams default to familiar patterns: dominant voices prevail, convergence happens prematurely, and friction becomes destructive.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: Unfacilitated group (one large shape dominating smaller shapes, premature convergence shown as a funnel closing too early). Right side: Facilitated group (equal-sized shapes arranged in balance, funnel held open to allow exploration). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the structural supports that hold the space open and balanced.
