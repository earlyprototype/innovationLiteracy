# E09 — Facilitation: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Facilitation vs Presentation

### Body
Asking, not telling. The primary tool is the question.

### Visual Direction
Contrast layout. Facilitation: enabling the process, not directing the outcome. Presentation: telling people what to know. Facilitation is not a personality trait — it is a developable capability with identifiable practices. The facilitation gap: groups cannot reliably self-facilitate, especially in early stages. Even the best processes and most capable participants underperform without it.
