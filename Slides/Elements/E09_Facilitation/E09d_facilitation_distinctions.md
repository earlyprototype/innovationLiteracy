# E09d — Facilitation: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Facilitating vs Participating

### Body
A participant contributes content. A facilitator guides the process that allows content to emerge.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Participant (shown as a source of raw material, feeding blocks into a system). Right: Facilitator (shown not carrying blocks, but operating the switches, gates, and flow of the system itself). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) clarifies the distinct nature of the two roles.
