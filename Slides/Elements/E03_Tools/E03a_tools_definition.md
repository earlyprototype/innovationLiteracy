# E03a — Tools: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What are Tools?

### Body
Physical objects and digital technologies that enable innovation work. The means of execution. 

### Visual Direction
Clean, structural arrangement of essential tools. Digital representations (Mural board icon, AI prompt box) alongside physical representations (sticky notes, markers). Arranged precisely on a grid. Neon yellow (#DFFF00) lines connecting the tools to the process framework, showing where they plug in. Not a messy desk, but an organized toolkit.
