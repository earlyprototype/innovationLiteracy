# E10b — Governance: Why It Matters

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Governance?

### Body
Even the best innovation practice fails without organisational alignment. It provides insulation from operational expectations and formal agreement on value.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: Innovation practice crushed by operational gravity (a fragile shape being broken by heavy, misaligned gears). Right side: Innovation practice protected by governance (a structural shield or buffer zone allowing the fragile shape to operate while remaining connected to the heavy gears). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the protective infrastructure.
