# E10a — Governance: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What is Governance?

### Body
The bidirectional communication infrastructure between innovation practice and the broader organisation. The bridge between the work and the system.

### Visual Direction
Structural diagram of a two-way bridge or conduit. Neon yellow (#DFFF00) arrows showing information flowing in both directions between a smaller bounded system (innovation practice) and a larger enveloping system (the organisation). Clean architectural diagram style representing structural connection.
