# E10 — Governance: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Governance vs Environment vs Resources

### Body
Agreement. Conditions. Commitments.

### Visual Direction
Three-way relationship. Governance: the legitimising structure (formal mechanisms that protect conditions). Environment: the container (physical, temporal, cultural conditions). Resources: the tangible commitments (what flows from governance agreement). Governance establishes shared understanding before resource requirements are specified. Bidirectional: inward (purpose, permission) and outward (evidence, needs).
