# E10 — Governance: In Practice

## Slide Content

### Heading
Governance Failures You Diagnosed

### Body
Wrong metrics. Premature results demands. Penalising productive failure.

### Visual Direction
The Measurement Game reframed as a governance insight. Wrong metrics applied to innovation are a governance failure — stage-inappropriate measurement crushes innovation. Leadership endorses innovation enthusiastically then systematically undermines it by reclaiming time, demanding premature results, or penalising productive failure. The innovation charter is the primary mechanism: written agreement defining scope, time, resources, decision-making, behaviours, and outcome capture.
