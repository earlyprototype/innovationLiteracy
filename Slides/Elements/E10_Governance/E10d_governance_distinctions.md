# E10d — Governance: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Governance vs Management

### Body
Management focuses on how the work is executed. Governance focuses on why the work exists and how it connects to the organisation.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Management (shown as gears interlocking, focusing on internal efficiency and process steps). Right: Governance (shown as a radar or compass, aligning the entire mechanism with a distant target or larger system). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the outward-facing, structural alignment aspect of governance.
