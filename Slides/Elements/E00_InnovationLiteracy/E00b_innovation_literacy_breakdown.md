# E00 — Innovation Literacy: What / How / Why

## Slide Content

### Heading
Three Questions, Eleven Elements

### Body
What innovation requires. How it gets executed. Why organisations support it.

### Visual Direction
Three-column layout. The What (Process, Interventions, Tools) — activity. The How (Skills, Mindset, Behaviours, Environment, Collaboration, Facilitation) — execution. The Why (Governance, Resources) — organisational capability. Visual emphasis on interdependence: process capability without organisational capability gets crushed; management structures without process capability produce innovation theatre.
