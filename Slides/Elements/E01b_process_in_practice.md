### Visual Direction
The Wallet Project phases mapped to Design Thinking. Each phase labelled with what participants did: interviewed partners (Empathise), wrote How Might We statements (Define), generated ideas (Ideate), built with household materials (Prototype), showed partner via camera (Test). Grounded, recognisable, experience-first.
