# E02d — Interventions: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Interventions vs Tools

### Body
Tools are what you use. Interventions are how you are instructed to use them.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Tools—shown as structural outlines of physical or digital items (e.g., Mural, pencil, canvas). Right: Interventions—shown as deliberate actions or rules (e.g., "sketch, don't write", "5 minutes"). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the rule/action applied to the tool. Technical annotations clarify the boundary: tool is inert, intervention makes it active.
