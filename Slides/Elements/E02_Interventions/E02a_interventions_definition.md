# E02a — Interventions: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What are Interventions?

### Body
Deliberate, structured actions at specific points. Pattern interrupts. Instructions or frameworks that shape, test, or redirect outcomes.

### Visual Direction
Lightning bolt or disruption motif. Abstract representation of a process flow being deliberately interrupted or redirected by a tactical action (neon yellow shape). Emphasise precision—interventions happen at strategic moments, not randomly. Clean architectural diagram style.
