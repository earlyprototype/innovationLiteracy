# E02b — Interventions: Why It Matters

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Interventions?

### Body
Prevents stalling. Stops solution-jumping. Reduces uncertainty. De-risks innovation by generating evidence before committing resources.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: failure modes without interventions (teams stalling, jumping to conclusions, defending ideas). Right side: clarity with interventions (structured progress, evidence-based decisions). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) used to show how interventions cut through ambiguity. Minimal elements, clear contrast.
