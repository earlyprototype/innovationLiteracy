# E01 — Process: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Process vs Interventions

### Body
Process is the map. Interventions are the moves.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Process: the five-phase structure providing direction. Interventions: the deliberate actions ("dig deeper", "3 minutes, go", "don't defend") that move you through it. Non-linear: phases revisited as understanding deepens. Human-centred throughout. Failure is data — each iteration narrows uncertainty.
