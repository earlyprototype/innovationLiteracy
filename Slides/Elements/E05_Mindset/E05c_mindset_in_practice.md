# E05c — Mindset: In Practice

## Slide Content

### Heading
Core Orientations

### Body
Curiosity. Tolerance for Uncertainty. Challenging Assumptions. Perspective-Taking. Growth Orientation. Bias Towards Action.

### Visual Direction
Hexagonal or multi-node diagram showing the core orientations. Each node is a facet of the overall mindset. Neon yellow (#DFFF00) geometric shapes anchor each orientation. Arranged as an interconnected system, demonstrating that these are active stances, not passive traits.
