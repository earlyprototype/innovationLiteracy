# E05d — Mindset: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Mindset vs Personality

### Body
Personality is who you are. Mindset is how you orient to a specific context.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Personality (shown as a solid, unchangeable block or core). Right: Mindset (shown as a rotatable dial or adjustable aperture). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the adjustable, context-dependent nature of mindset. Technical annotations clarify the boundary.
