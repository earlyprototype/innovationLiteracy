# E05 — Mindset: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Mindset vs Personality

### Body
Not fixed. Not innate. Shaped by environment and practice.

### Visual Direction
Contrast layout. Mindset shifts with context — curious and exploratory in personal life, risk-averse at work. The difference is environment. Organisations claiming to value innovation while punishing failure systematically suppress the mindsets innovation requires. Direct connection to Environment (E07) and Governance (E10).
