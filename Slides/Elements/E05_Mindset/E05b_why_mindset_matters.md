# E05b — Mindset: Why It Matters

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Mindset?

### Body
Innovation requires risk, ambiguity, and persistence. Punitive environments suppress these orientations. Culture shapes mindset.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: Rigid environment (boxes, right angles, restricted paths). Right side: Adaptive environment (open pathways, flexible structures). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights how the structure of the environment directly influences the shape of the mindset within it. Minimal elements, clear contrast.
