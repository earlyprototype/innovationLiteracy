# E05a — Mindset: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What is Mindset?

### Body
Habitual patterns of thinking and cognitive orientations. Not personality. Not fixed. Shaped by experience, environment, and practice.

### Visual Direction
Abstract representation of a cognitive lens or filter. A central "eye" or "lens" shape that bends or processes incoming lines of data. Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the adaptability of the lens. Emphasise that mindset is a developed orientation, not a hardcoded trait. Clean architectural diagram style.
