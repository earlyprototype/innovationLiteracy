# E01c — Process: In Practice

## Slide Content

### Heading
Process Frameworks

### Body
Design Thinking: human-centred, iterative. Double Diamond: diverge-converge, twice. Different frameworks for different contexts—all share gated progression and learning loops.

### Visual Direction
Two frameworks side-by-side. Left: Design Thinking—five-phase circle (Empathise, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test) with iteration arrows. Right: Double Diamond—two diamonds showing Discover-Define, Develop-Deliver. Visual similarity emphasised: both non-linear, both gated, both iterative. Labels minimal, shapes clear, structural parallels evident.
