# E01b — Process: Why It Matters

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Process?

### Body
Team alignment. Coherence and efficiency. Expectation setting. Enables stage-appropriate interventions. Prevents solution-jumping.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left: chaos without process—arrows everywhere, team misaligned, solutions proposed before problems understood. Right: clarity with process—structured flow, team synchronized, evidence-based progression. Clean contrast, minimal elements, clear before/after narrative.
