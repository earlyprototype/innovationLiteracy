# E07a — Environment: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What is Environment?

### Body
The physical spaces, temporal boundaries, and cultural conditions that either support or undermine innovation behaviours. The container for the work.

### Visual Direction
Structural diagram of a "container." A neon yellow (#DFFF00) bounding box or framework protecting a set of activities occurring inside it, shielding them from external pressures (represented by grey arrows or weight pushing down). Clean architectural metaphor for boundaries.
