# E07 — Environment: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Environment vs Governance

### Body
Environment is the container. Governance is what protects it.

### Visual Direction
Nested structure. Environment: physical/virtual spaces, temporal boundaries (protected time), cultural conditions (norms and expectations). Governance: formal mechanisms that create organisational agreement to maintain those conditions. Without governance, operational culture dominates through gravitational pull of the urgent over the important. Leadership behaviour is an environmental factor.
