# E07d — Environment: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Innovation Time vs Spare Time

### Body
Innovation time is a deliberate, protected temporal boundary. Spare time is an abdication to operational gravity.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Protected Time (shown as a solid, ring-fenced block in a schedule, impenetrable by external arrows). Right: Spare Time (shown as fragmented, crumbling pieces of time being crushed or absorbed by larger operational blocks). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the strength of the intentional boundary.
