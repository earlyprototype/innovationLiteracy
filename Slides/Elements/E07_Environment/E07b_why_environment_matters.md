# E07b — Environment: Why It Matters

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Environment?

### Body
Operations demand predictability and convergence. Innovation demands exploration and divergence. They cannot occupy the same space under the same rules.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: the crushing weight of operations (heavy blocks, tight constraints, linear flows dominating delicate exploration). Right side: protected space (a distinct zone where non-linear, exploratory paths are given room to expand). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the necessity of the boundary.
