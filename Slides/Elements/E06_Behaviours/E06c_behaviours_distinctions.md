# E06 — Behaviours: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Behaviours vs Mindset

### Body
Behaviours are what you do. Mindset is how you think.

### Visual Direction
Two-column comparison. Behaviours: observable, practisable, collective. Mindset: internal, cognitive, individual. Active listening is the foundational innovation behaviour — underpins every phase. Behaviours produce full value when practised collectively and established as explicit shared norms.
