# E06d — Behaviours: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Active Listening vs Waiting to Speak

### Body
Active listening is processing another's perspective. Waiting to speak is preparing your rebuttal.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Active Listening (shown as an open receptor, taking in information, processing loop). Right: Waiting to Speak (shown as a closed system, projecting outward, bouncing incoming information away). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the difference in information flow. Technical annotations clarify the boundary.
