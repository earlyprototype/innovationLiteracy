# E06b — Behaviours: Why They Matter

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Behaviours?

### Body
Mindset without behaviour is aspiration. Behaviour without mindset is performance. Innovation requires both, practised as team norms.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: The gap (a thought bubble disconnected from hands, or individuals acting in isolation). Right side: The connection (alignment between thought and shared action, a team moving in sync). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the bridge between intent and action. Minimal elements, clear contrast.
