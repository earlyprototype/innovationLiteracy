# E06a — Behaviours: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What are Behaviours?

### Body
Observable, practicable actions that translate mindset into practice. Habits developed through repetition and established as shared norms.

### Visual Direction
Visualisation of action loops. A neon yellow (#DFFF00) sequence showing mindset (an abstract thought bubble) transforming into behaviour (an action arrow) creating an outcome. The emphasis is on visibility—behaviours are what we can see, not just what we think. Clean architectural diagram style.
