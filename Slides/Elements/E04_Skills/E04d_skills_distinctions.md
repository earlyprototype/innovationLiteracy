# E04d — Skills: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Skills vs Mindset

### Body
Skills are the capabilities you deploy. Mindset is the attitude that allows them to be deployed.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Skills (shown as a toolkit or set of mechanical gears representing specific capabilities). Right: Mindset (shown as the environment or lens through which those gears operate). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the distinction: one is the engine, the other is the fuel/environment.
