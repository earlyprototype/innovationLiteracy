# E04 — Skills and Competencies: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Skills vs Mindset

### Body
Skills are what you can do. Mindset is how you approach.

### Visual Direction
Two-column comparison. Skills: practised capabilities (observable, developable). Mindset: cognitive orientations (curiosity, tolerance for uncertainty). Both required — analytical and creative thinking are not opposites but alternate within the process. WEF Future of Jobs data: these same skills are the fastest-growing competency requirements globally.
