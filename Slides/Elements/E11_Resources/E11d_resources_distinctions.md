# E11d — Resources: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Secured vs Negotiated

### Body
Operational resources are secured. Innovation resources are continuously negotiated.

### Visual Direction
Side-by-side comparison. Left: Secured Resources (shown as a locked vault or fixed pipe). Right: Negotiated Resources (shown as a dynamic valve or balance scale requiring continuous input and adjustment). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the active, ongoing effort required to keep the innovation resource valve open against opposing pressure.
