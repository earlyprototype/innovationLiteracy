# E11 — Resources: Key Distinctions

## Slide Content

### Heading
Resources vs Governance

### Body
Resources are what flows. Governance is why they flow.

### Visual Direction
Relationship diagram. Resources: tangible commitments (time, budget, people, materials). Governance: the agreement that validates and protects those commitments. Resources are never "secured" — continuously contested against operational priorities. Five layers of innovation value for audience-appropriate justification: capability, learning, engagement, pipeline, strategic positioning. The evidence base is experiential: "I need X because without it, Element Y breaks."
