# E11a — Resources: Definition

## Slide Content

### Heading
What are Resources?

### Body
Time, budget, people, and materials. Inherently vulnerable to operational gravity. Continuously contested, requiring ongoing negotiation and defence.

### Visual Direction
Structural diagram showing resources not as a static pile, but as a flow that must be actively managed and protected. Neon yellow (#DFFF00) lines representing resource streams (time, budget, people) flowing into the innovation container, with defensive barriers keeping operational pressures from severing the flow.
