# E11b — Resources: Why They Matter

## Slide Content

### Heading
Why Resources?

### Body
Without them, innovation operates on goodwill. The absence of resources signals a lack of true priority, reducing innovation to theatre.

### Visual Direction
Split visual. Left side: Innovation Theatre (a shiny facade or hollow wireframe with no internal substance or fuel). Right side: Sustained Innovation (a solid, functioning engine actively powered by distinct resource streams). Neon yellow (#DFFF00) highlights the crucial difference: actual fuel vs empty structure.
