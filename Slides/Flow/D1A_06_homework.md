# D1A — Homework Assignment

## Slide Content

### Heading
Three Tasks for the Week

### Body
Read. Map. Notice.

### Visual Direction
Clean three-part structure. Each task distinct and actionable. Calendar/week-gap motif suggesting bridge between Day 1 and Day 2.

**1. Read** — Design Thinking framework document. Full process overview. 15-20 minutes.

**2. Map** — Use Problem Space Map to analyse your challenge. 30-45 minutes. Apply to something real from your context.

**3. Notice** — Identify 2-3 moments in your work where Design Thinking elements would change outcomes. Bring specific examples to Day 2.
